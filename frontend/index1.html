About the Project

Our innovative system integrates an ESP32-powered medical kiosk with a powerful web-based dashboard. The kiosk is equipped with advanced sensors to collect vital patient data in real-time, securely transmitting it to our cloud server.

Healthcare professionals can access a comprehensive dashboard to remotely monitor patient vitals, view AI-driven disease predictions, and make informed decisions. Upon doctor approval, the system facilitates automated, precise medication dispensing directly from the kiosk.
